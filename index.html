Overview

DOOM (2016) is a fast-paced first-person shooter developed by id Software and published by Bethesda Softworks. Made as a reboot to the original DOOM franchise since the release of DOOM 3, The franchise makes a return and makes it a grand and bloody one at that. It brings back the fast-paced experience of the original DOOM games and increases it by ten-fold, by adding many different levels, guns and even a new level of verticality never seen before in previous DOOM games.

Key Information

  • Developer: id Software
  • Publisher: Bethesda Softworks
  • Engine: id Tech 6
  • Release Date: May 13, 2016
  • Platforms: PC, PS4, Xbox One, Nintendo Switch

Creative Team

Game Director: Marty Stratton
Composer: Mick Gordon
Lead Programmer: Billy Khan
Lead Designer: Hugo Martin

Main Characters

  • Doom Slayer: The silent and chaotic protagonist, hellbent on killing any demon he encounters.
  • Dr. Samuel Hayden: A cyborg scientist with questionable ethics involving argent energy.
  • Olivia Pierce: Head of UAC research, responsible for opening the gates of Hell.

Reviews

DOOM (2016) received critical acclaim for its gameplay, graphics, and absolute near perfection of a soundtrack composed by the legendary Mick Gordon. IGN gave it a 9.0/10, calling it "fast, punchy, and genuinely fun to play." GameSpot praised its "relentless pacing and ultra-violent combat."
